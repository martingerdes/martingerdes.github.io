In conjunction with [publishing version 1.3](/blog/2026/version1_3) of the book, I am also moving the URL this website can be found at. Mirroring the English book title "Futurities", the website URL is from now on [https://futurities.net](https://futurities.net) (instead of futurevisions.world).

Aside from matching the book title, I like it both because it is shorter, and because .net is a better known top-level domain (compared to .world). Even though ".net" traditionally just stands for the internet, I think it fits here thematically as well: the book presents a network of interconnected ideas, after all.

futurevisions.world will continue to redirect to futurities.net for a while yet, but please update any bookmarks or references to it you might have.
